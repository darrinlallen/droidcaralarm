Droid Car Alarm

Provisional Patent Application - Intelligent System to Prevent Vehicle Theft and Hot Car Death

Abstract

Droid Car Alarm is an innovative car security system combining artificial intelligence, cameras, and audio alerts to simultaneously prevent vehicle theft and hot car deaths involving children or pets. This system closely monitors the interior environment and vehicle surroundings to proactively protect both the vehicle and its occupants.

Background and Objectives

  • Prevent child and pet deaths caused by heat exposure inside vehicles using AI-based temperature, motion, and presence detection.
  • Provide immediate alerts and in-car voice warnings to deter emergencies.
  • Incorporate a comprehensive state-of-the-art car alarm system to prevent theft, including motion sensors and engine immobilizers.
  • Enable remote monitoring and real-time notifications via smartphone integration.

Detailed Description of the Invention

  • AI-driven cameras monitor vehicle interior and exterior for occupant presence and suspicious activity.
  • Temperature sensors combined with AI analyze heat levels to detect dangerous conditions inside the vehicle.
  • Integrated speaker system issues pre-recorded or dynamic warnings inside the vehicle to alert occupants.
  • Vehicle theft prevention uses multi-factor sensor input to detect tampering or unauthorized entry.
  • Mobile app integration enables real-time alerts, remote arming/disarming, and live camera feeds.

Claims

  • An AI-enabled car alarm system that simultaneously prevents vehicle theft and hot car death.
  • The use of integrated cameras and AI algorithms to detect occupant presence and unsafe heat conditions.
  • Real-time alerting through mobile notifications and in-car voice warnings.
  • A comprehensive theft deterrent system including motion detection, tampering sensors, and engine immobilizers.
